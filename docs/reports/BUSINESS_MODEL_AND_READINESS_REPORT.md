## Main conclusion

TreasuryOS is commercially credible **now** as a paid-pilot and design-partner
business. It is not yet a broad fully mature regulated production platform.

The recommended near-term model is:

1. paid pilot
2. annual platform conversion
3. services and premium module expansion
