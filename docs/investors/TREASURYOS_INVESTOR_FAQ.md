# TreasuryOS Investor FAQ

## What is TreasuryOS?

TreasuryOS is a compliance-first operating system for institutional
digital-asset treasury. It unifies entity and wallet controls, transaction
screening, case management, governance-aware workflow, reporting, audit logs,
and AI-assisted human review.

## Is the product live?

Yes. TreasuryOS is live in a controlled pilot posture with an active dashboard
and API, live transaction-case workflows, reporting, audit logging, and a live
AI advisory flow for transaction-case analysis.

## Is TreasuryOS already on mainnet?

No. The current live posture is testnet-active on Solana. Mainnet remains
intentionally gated behind external audit progress and governance readiness.

## What does the AI layer do today?

The AI layer generates structured advisory analysis for transaction cases. It is
operator-triggered and advisory-only. It does not approve transactions, bypass
policy, or access signers.

## Why is advisory-only AI important?

It lets TreasuryOS improve reviewer productivity without undermining control.
That is the right posture for institutional workflows where accountability must
stay with human operators.

## Who is the product for?

TreasuryOS is designed for institutional teams that need governed digital-asset
operations, especially treasury, compliance, operations, and risk stakeholders.

## How is this different from wallets, custody, or multisig?

Those tools solve pieces of the stack. TreasuryOS is focused on the operational
control layer that connects workflow, review, governance, reporting, and
auditable execution context.

## Why Solana?

Solana provides the performance and ecosystem needed for digital-asset treasury
use cases, while TreasuryOS focuses on the institutional operating layer above
the chain.

## What is not complete yet?

The main open items are:

- external protocol audit engagement and findings cycle
- production KYC rollout
- production banking integration work
- broader AI expansion beyond the first live workflow
- gated mainnet readiness

## How do you plan to monetize?

The intended model is enterprise infrastructure pricing around platform access,
workflow value, integrations, and premium operational modules. Exact pricing and
packaging should be finalized before external fundraising materials claim them
as locked.

## What should investors underwrite?

Investors should underwrite TreasuryOS as the institutional control plane for
digital-asset treasury: a product that can sit between compliance teams,
treasury teams, governance workflows, and blockchain infrastructure.

## What is the biggest strategic advantage?

TreasuryOS combines three things that are usually separated:

1. compliance and treasury workflow
2. governance-aware operational design
3. AI-assisted human review embedded directly in the workflow

## What is the biggest execution risk?

The main risk is trust expansion, not product existence. The key milestones are
audit completion, production integrations, and proving that the first AI surface
delivers clear operator value.
