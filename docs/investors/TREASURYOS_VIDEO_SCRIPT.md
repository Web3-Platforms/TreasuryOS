# TreasuryOS Investor Video Script

Use these scripts for short async investor intros or a more complete founder-led
overview video.

## Version 1 - 60-second intro video

### Recording goal

Create a quick video for warm intros, email follow-ups, or investor DMs.

| Time | Visual | Script |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0:00-0:08 | Founder on camera | Hi, I am building TreasuryOS, a compliance-first operating system for institutional digital-asset treasury. |
| 0:08-0:18 | Product screenshot or dashboard hero | We help institutions manage entity and wallet controls, transaction screening, case review, governance workflows, reporting, and audit logs in one platform. |
| 0:18-0:30 | Transaction case page | We also have a live AI advisory layer that helps human reviewers analyze transaction cases on demand, without giving AI approval authority or signer access. |

| 0:42-0:55 | Founder back on camera | Our next phase is external audit, production integrations, and broader institutional readiness. |
| 0:55-1:00 | Simple CTA slide | If this overlaps with your focus in fintech, regtech, or institutional crypto infrastructure, I would love to show you the product. |

## Delivery notes

- Keep the tone calm, credible, and operational.
- Show the product wherever possible instead of relying only on slides.
- Do not add claims about customers, revenue, or audits unless those numbers are confirmed.
- End with a specific CTA: a live demo, product walkthrough, or diligence call.
