# TreasuryOS Future Work

This document frames the roadmap for investors using the current verified
product posture.

## Roadmap principle

TreasuryOS is sequencing the roadmap around trust and institutional readiness.
That means the next milestones are not random feature additions; they are the
specific steps that make the platform more credible, more deployable, and more
valuable to regulated operators.

## Future work by phase

| Phase | Current state | Next work | Why it matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AI rollout closure | AI advisory is live for transaction cases in controlled mode | run canaries, review operator feedback, confirm live model posture | proves the first AI workflow is useful before expansion |
| External trust and assurance | audit preparation is complete | engage the audit firm, complete the review, remediate findings | increases buyer confidence and supports later production posture |
| Production connectivity | production KYC and banking paths are not complete yet | finish Sumsub production setup and banking credential + mTLS rollout | bridges pilot product into real institutional operations |
| AI expansion | AI is live only on transaction-case analysis | expand to other read-only surfaces after the first path is stable | increases workflow leverage without weakening controls |
| Solana production posture | testnet-active sync is live | keep mainnet gated until audit and governance readiness are complete | preserves institutional trust and reduces rollout risk |

## Near-term investor framing

The near-term story is:

1. prove controlled usefulness in the live pilot
2. convert audit preparation into external validation
3. connect the remaining production infrastructure

This is the most credible path for an institutional product because it shows
discipline instead of chasing headline features prematurely.

## Mid-term investor framing

After the trust layer is stronger, TreasuryOS can expand in three directions:

- deeper institutional workflow coverage
- more read-only AI support across operations
- broader production deployment posture once audit and governance gates are satisfied

## How to talk about future work

Use language like:

- "Our next phase is trust expansion."
- "We are sequencing audit, production integrations, and controlled AI expansion."
- "Mainnet readiness is gated intentionally rather than rushed."

Avoid language like:

- "Everything is almost done."
- "We just need marketing."
- "AI will replace the compliance team."

## What investors should see in the roadmap

The roadmap is valuable because each milestone compounds:

- audit improves trust
- production integrations improve usability
- AI expansion improves reviewer productivity
- gated on-chain progression improves credibility

That creates a stronger institutional platform instead of a loose collection of
features.
