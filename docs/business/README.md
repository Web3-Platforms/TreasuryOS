## Important messaging guardrails

- Say **pilot-live** or **controlled pilot**, not broad regulated production.
- Say **testnet-active Solana sync**, not mainnet live.
- Say **AI advisory-only**, not autonomous approvals.
- Say **external audit prepared**, not externally audited.
- Sell **paid pilot and design-partner value first**, not oversized enterprise
  promises that depend on unfinished KYC, banking, or mainnet lanes.
