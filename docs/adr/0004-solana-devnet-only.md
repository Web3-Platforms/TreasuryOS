# ADR 0004: Solana Devnet Only

## Status

Accepted

## Decision

The MVP only targets Solana devnet for wallet whitelist and related chain synchronization work.

## Rationale

- keeps pilot testing cheap and reversible
- avoids production custody and mainnet risk too early
- is enough to prove approval workflow behavior

## Consequence

Mainnet, production signing controls, and custody/HSM strategy remain Real App work.
